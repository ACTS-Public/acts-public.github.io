Overview



The Advisory Committee on Trustworthy Systems (ACTS) is constituted from representatives from, in alphabetical order, Academia, Government, Professional Bodies, Regulators, Standards Development Organisations (SDO), and Trade Bodies.



Its primary Responsibilities are the curation of the Trustworthy Systems Framework (TFSr), and associated Training, Education, and Awareness (TEA) materials.



News



A new Version 5.0 of the Trustworthy Systems Framework (TSFr) is currently under development, with a Draft for Public Consultation (DPC) is expected to be launched during 3Q2025.
